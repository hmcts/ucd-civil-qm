Agreement from the other parties

You’ll need to pay a fee to make an application.

The fee amount depends on whether the other parties have agreed to you making this application. The other parties are the other side or sides involved in your case.

Getting agreement

Asking the other parties to agree is optional. If they’ve agreed, you do not need to have proof of this.

If you’re not able to contact the other parties, or you’ve attempted to contact them but have not had a response, select 'No'.

If the other parties have agreed

The application fee will be reduced, unless this is an application to:

  • set aside (remove) a judgment
  • vary a judgment
  • reconsider an order

There may also be no need for a hearing to make a decision on the application. You’ll see the final application fee amount before you pay.
