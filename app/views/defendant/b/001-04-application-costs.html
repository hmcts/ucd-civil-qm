Application costs

When a judge makes a decision on your application, they'll decide who should pay the costs.

If your application is successful, the other parties may be ordered to pay back your costs associated with the application.

Costs can include amounts like:

  • your application fee
  • loss of earnings and travel costs if you have to attend a hearing

You’ll still need to pay the application fee when you submit the application.

The other parties will be able to see your answer to this question and respond to it.
