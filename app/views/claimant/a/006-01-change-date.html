Manage your hearing

Change the hearing date

To apply to change the hearing date (known as a adjournment) you will need to apply to adjourn the hearing.

You will need to say why you need the date of the hearing changed and if the other parties agree to the change.

You may need to attend a hearing to discuss your application to change the date.

Fees

If you are applying to change the hearing date less than 14 days before the hearing, it can cost up to £275, the amount may be lower if you are making more than one application.

You do not have to pay for an application to change the hearing date if you apply more than 14 days before the date of the hearing.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
