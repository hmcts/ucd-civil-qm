Understand what happens if claim is not paid

The defendant has 28 days to respond once they receive the claim (if both parties agree, the 28 days can be extended).

They can decide to:

  • pay the full amount
  • pay a different amount (if they think they owe less)
  • defend the claim (if they do not think they owe any money)

If they offer to pay a different amount and that is rejected, there may be a hearing where they will have to give more information. The claimant will be asked to attend the hearing.

If the defendant does not pay the full amount in time

If they do not pay in time, the claimant will be given the option torequest a county court judgment.

Anything else

If you still need help on your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
