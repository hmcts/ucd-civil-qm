Settle a claim

If the claim is paid or you agree the balance is settled

Update the claim in the online service to say the claim is settled, do that by selecting Tell us you’ve settled the claim.

You can also email contactocmc@justice.gov.uk to say the claim is settled, you should include:

  • your name
  • the claim number
  • the date the final payment cleared in your account

Claims settled through a small claims mediatorv

If a mediator settled your claim, the mediator will update your case record for you.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
