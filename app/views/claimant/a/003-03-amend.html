Amend the claim details

You can change the details of your claim, including:

  • the reasons for the claim
  • the claim amount
  • the person you are claiming against

You must apply to amend the claim details, do this by making an application, you will have to pay a fee of £119.

If you are changing the amount of the claim, this could change the court fee you have to pay. Check the fees list to make sure you pay the correct fee.

If the defendant’s address is wrong

You can send an amended claim form, with the correct address written in red ink. Email the amended form to contactocmc@justice.gov.uk.

To update your contact details

To update your contact details, raise a query.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
