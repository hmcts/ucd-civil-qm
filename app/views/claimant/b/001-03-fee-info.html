Change a hearing date

To apply to change a hearing date, the application fee is £275. If you’ll be selecting multiple applications, this fee may be reduced.

If the hearing date that you want to change is more than 14 days away from the date you make this application, you will not need to pay a fee. If you add another application, you’ll still have to pay the fee for this additional application.

If the hearing is less than 14 days away, the fee above applies.

The court may not agree to change the hearing, even if both parties have agreed.

The fee will need to be paid once you’ve created the application. If you’re eligible, you may be able to apply for help with fees.
