Change hearing details

You can apply to change the details of the hearing, such as:

  • the location
  • the hearing type - in person or video hearing

You have to apply to the court to ask for the court to consider changing the hearing details.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
