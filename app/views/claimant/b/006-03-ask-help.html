Manage hearing details

Ask for help and support during your hearing

You can ask for help and support during your hearing.

For example, this could be

  • ramps or accessible toilets
  • a hearing loop
  • forms in large print
  • guidance in audio or easy read formats

To ask for support at your hearing you have to contact the court (opens in a new window) where the hearing is being held.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
