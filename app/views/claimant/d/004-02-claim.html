Send claim evidence

To upload evidence to your case, select upload claim evidence .

When you are saving the files, make sure the file names make it clear what the file is, for example: yourname-evidencetype.doc.

If you want to upload documents after the upload deadline, you need to ask for more time to do what is required by a court order.

Anything else

If you still need help with your claim, you can send a message to the court.

It takes at least 10 days for the court to respond to a message.
